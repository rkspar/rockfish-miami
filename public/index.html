About Rockfish

We are a family owned & operated restaurant offering excellent quality with Fresh Seafood, Steaks, Pasta's, Salads, Sandwiches, Burgers, Wings & more. Our range of menu choices gives our guests a maximum selection like our made from scratch Maryland style Crab Cakes, Shrimp Garlic Pasta & Fresh Fish selections like Mahi-Mahi, Tuna or Salmon. Or how about a Coconut Shrimp Salad, Buffalo Chicken Sandwich or maybe our juicy Burger made with Black Angus Beef like our "Bomb" Burger with everything on it? And, if that's not enough we are always offering creative rotating specials posted daily on the in-store Special Board.


Our restaurant is designed to make you feel "at home" with a comfortable and relaxed dining atmosphere. From the playing of the best Classic Rock for your listening pleasure to the big sport games on one of our Flat Screen TV's. To the large selection of Beer & Wine, table or counter/bar seating to a very comfortable ambience with some great "music memorabilia" from Jimi Hendrix to Pink Floyd. We want our guests not only enjoy a wonderful meal but be sure to enjoy their time at Rock Fish Grill.
